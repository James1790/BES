Packages We Offer

At Bryant Exterior Solutions, we take pride in creating beautiful, safe, and energy-efficient holiday displays that stand up to Midwest winters. We use Minleon C9 SMD LED bulbs that shine brighter and last longer than typical store-bought lights. Our lights are professionally installed with SPT-1 wiring, flex clips that prevent any home damage, and outdoor light sensor timers—all designed to work flawlessly, even in temperatures as low as -20°F. With Bryant Exterior Solutions, you can trust that every detail is handled to bring your holiday vision to life.
